When ONE gives TWO gets HAPPY

What began as a shared dream between two friends, Ashok and Guna, has grown into a heartfelt mission to bring hope, help, and happiness to those in need. Our journey started with small acts of kindness, driven by a deep desire to make a positive difference in our community.

Today, through HFHL Trust, we continue to work together to provide food, education, and support to the underprivileged, believing that even the smallest step can create a big impact. Our story wouldn’t be complete without the unwavering encouragement and active participation of our friends, family members, relatives, and many kind-hearted well-wishers. Their support has been the backbone of our growth, turning our small vision into a larger movement of compassion and care.

Above all, we believe it is by the grace of God that we are able to do all these things. Every step forward, every smile we bring, and every life we touch is a blessing, and we remain grateful to God for guiding our path and giving us the strength to serve.

DONATIONS

How Our Monthly Contribution Works (₹199) HFHL Trust is built on simple, steady support.

Each month, our friends, family members, relatives, and well-wishers contribute ₹199 as trust members. This small, consistent amount—multiplied across many caring people—creates a reliable fund we can plan with every month.

How We Decide and Use the Funds

1️⃣ Collect & Record: We collect the monthly ₹199 contributions and record every entry for transparency.

2️⃣ Plan Together: At the start of each month, our core team (founded by Ashok and Guna) reviews needs and suggestions from members.

3️⃣ Allocate Wisely: We set a simple plan for that month, prioritizing urgent and high-impact activities.

4️⃣ Execute & Report: We carry out the plan with volunteers/partners, then share updates, photos, and a short impact summary.

Where Your ₹199 Goes

✔️Food Donation: Cooked meals and grocery kits for the homeless, elderly, and low-income families.

✔️Educational Assistance: School supplies, fees support in special cases, mentorship sessions, and small learning grants.

✔️Healthcare Support: Basic medical camps, essential medicines in urgent cases, and connections to affordable care.

✔️Social Welfare & Relief: Seasonal drives (blankets, sanitary kits), emergency relief, and community development activities.
